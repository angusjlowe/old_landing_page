About

It's a given that I'm passionate about technology, but what's less evident is my commitment to experiencing all the digital world has to offer: from Wordpress to Wix, Nodejs to Haskell, and Firebase to Heroku, every possible combination of platform, technology, and design is an open door to me, waiting to be explored.
